About

Hey there, I'm ángel and this is my little actuarial learning site.

wanna contribute?

you can...

  • give suggestions for the content itself

  • send in your old notes! as long as it’s legible and easy to read through, it’ll make a cool addition to the site

  • send pull requests or email patches for my dodgy code

  • get in touch and share your thoughts

reach out through github, sourcehut, or plain old email: angel@acsq.me.
